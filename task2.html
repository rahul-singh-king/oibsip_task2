Inspiring Biography of Sundar Pichai

Pichai Sundararajan (Sundar Pichai) was born on June 10, 1972 in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India into a middle class family. His father, Regunatha Pichai worked as a senior electrical engineer at General Electric Company (GEC) and he also owned a manufacturing plant which manufactured electrical components. His mother, Lakshmi worked as a stenographer and her job was to transcribe speech in shorthand. Sundar Pichai has a younger brother, Srinivasan Pichai. Why is Sundar Pichai inspiring? Sundar Pichai was very impressed by how a revolutionary idea and brilliant invention can unleash so many entrepreneurs to build startups which later revolutionised many industries in more than one way.
